Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new game?

Tap "Start New Game" on the main menu, then add players and set your target score (optional). Tap "Continue" to begin playing.

How do I join someone else's game?

Tap "Join Existing Game" and either:

  • Enter the 6-digit game code shown on the host's device, OR
  • Tap "Scan QR Code" to scan the code from the host's screen

Can I use this offline?

Yes! Local games (single device) work completely offline. Multiplayer games require an internet connection to sync scores across devices.

How many players can join a game?

Game Scorer supports 2 to 20+ players per game.

How do I edit players during a game?

If you're the host, tap the "Players" button in the top bar, then you can add or remove players mid-game.

What are the two win modes?

  • First Past the Post: First player to reach the target score wins immediately
  • Highest Score on Final Round: Everyone gets one more turn after someone reaches the target, then highest score wins
